About Viña del Mar

Viña del Mar is a coastal resort city in central Chile. It’s known for its gardens, beaches, and colonial architecture. One of the most popular tourist destinations in the country, the city offers a blend of modern life and natural beauty.

Local Attractions

  • Flower Clock (Reloj de Flores)
  • Castillo Wulff
  • Reñaca Beach
  • Viña del Mar Casino
