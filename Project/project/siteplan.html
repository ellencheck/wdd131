Website Planning Document

Site Name

Discover Chile: Sea, Land & Sky Adventures

This name reflects a dynamic and immersive travel experience that combines historic sailing voyages, scenic air tours, and cultural land excursions. It evokes excitement, freedom, and the spirit of exploration.

Optional domain: adventuretours360.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to promote a unique travel service offering themed adventure tours that include:

  • Sailing on a historic-style schooner
  • Scenic flights on small aircraft
  • Guided land tours in picturesque locations

The site will provide information, photos, booking options, and detailed itineraries for each type of tour.

Scenarios

  • How do I book a sailing and flight combo package?
  • What kind of aircraft and schooners are used for the tours?
  • Are these tours family-friendly and safe?
  • Can I see sample itineraries or customize my experience?

Color Schema

The colors are chosen to reflect a nautical and adventure tone:

  • Deep Blue (#2a4d69): Used for headings, navigation bar, footer background.
  • Sand Gold (#dac292): Used for accents, buttons, and hover effects.

Typography

  • Merriweather (serif): Used for headings and important quotes or testimonials.
  • Segoe UI (sans-serif): Used for body text and navigation for readability and modern appeal.
